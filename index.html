Training & placement ( KITSW )

Our TNP (Training and Placement) Cell provides comprehensive training for students skilled in coding, helping them sharpen their technical abilities.

The college regularly supplies a variety of coding challenges and encourages students daily, motivating them to excel in their studies and coding practice

To foster advanced coding skills, the TNP Cell identifies and supports exceptional programmers, organizing them into a specialized group known as "Power Programmers."
