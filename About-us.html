Our Motto The Training and Placement Cell (TPC) is committed to bridging the gap between academia and industry by preparing students for successful professional careers. Our mission is to equip students with the essential skills, knowledge, and confidence they need to excel in their chosen fields. With a focus on career development, the TPC offers a range of programs and resources, including workshops, internships, on-campus recruitment drives, and networking opportunities with industry experts. Our comprehensive training program emphasizes industry-relevant skills and coding practices, providing students with access to experienced trainers who mentor them towards professional success. Through regular coding challenges and real-world problem-solving exercises, students enhance their technical abilities and are consistently motivated to reach new heights. Furthermore, we foster the growth of top-performing students by forming an elite group of "Power Programmers" dedicated to specialized skill development. Together, these initiatives ensure that TPC graduates are well-prepared to meet the demands of a dynamic job market.
